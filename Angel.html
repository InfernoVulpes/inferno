Angels

This Species are Astomical being, with ageless beauty. They are Fair and noble. They all have beautiful white wings hanging from their back, this making them have the ability to fly.
Although if twins are born the second baby is killed, because they are not born with white wings but black wings instead. Those are called the dark angels.
